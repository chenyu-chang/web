Projects

Obstructive Coronary Artery Disease Detection Sep 2023 – Present

Used CNN techniques (Inception and Residual Networks) to predict blockages in the three major coronary arteries using 3D nuclear myocardial perfusion scan images (multi-label classification task).

  • Optimized Preprocessing: Enhanced the team’s existing MATLAB image preprocessing code to handle parallel processing of multiple data, reducing computation time by over 10 times.
  • Modular Model Development: Modularized the previously developed TensorFlow-based deep learning model, facilitating easier adjustments and improvements by the team.
  • Clinical Validation: Conducted cross-hospital clinical validation, performing statistical analysis to assess the impact of different population distributions on prediction results.

Coronary Artery Disease Prognosis Prediction Aug 2022 – Present

Used Transformer technology, to predict the improvement of the three major coronary arteries post-cardiac catheterization using only 3D nuclear myocardial perfusion scan images (multi-label classification task).

  • Model Development and Optimization: Developed and fine-tuned a Swin Transformer deep learning model based on PyTorch MONAI, improving the prediction AUC from 0.7 to over 0.8.
  • Clinical Validation and Model Adjustment: Conducted cross-hospital clinical validation, identifying overfitting issues in the training dataset. Made appropriate adjustments to the model size to address overfitting and enhance generalizability.

Lung CT Registration (See my github) Jan 2023 – Mar 2023

  • Algorithm Implementation: Implemented the image registration feature described in the paper.

Plaque Detection in Blood Vessel Sep 2021 – Aug 2022
